100% Custom Design

Bring your exact vision to life. Nobody else does the level of customization we do in the faux live wall space.

Our custom designs come in three types of moss: reindeer moss (the classic), bunn moss (a little bigger and bolder), or our forest combo (just add a little foliage in there).

Exclusiveness is key in our projects, each order is processed individually so any size, shape, or design can be manufactured and installed.

Our moss walls are created from preserved moss. Preserved moss is no longer alive and has been through a preservation process to keep it as close to it’s natural state as possible.
